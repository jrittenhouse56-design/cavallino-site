## The New Boardroom Standard
Boards are moving beyond compliance toward strategic impact. Three practical shifts:
1. Clear owner for strategy cadence  
2. Evidence-based compensation alignment  
3. Forward-looking risk and scenario work
> Focus on clarity, credibility, and consistency.
